About Us

At Epicenter we connect those with help to provide to those who need it during hard times. Register as a helper in your community, and let the people close to you share what you have in abundance. Do you have access to clean water, an extra bed, or some outgrown clothes? Share it on Epicenter!


Verified Helpers

To ensure the legitimacy of helpers we use a verification system. Newly registered helpers sends us proof of their identity and ability to help, and we verify them as soon as possible. We allow people to register as helpers either way, but we encourage you to go through the verification process.
